# Phase 0 Checklist (Definition)

## Decisions to lock
- Brand voice: friendly, helpful.
- Pricing: free only.
- Privacy: no data collected.
- Support channel: email (support@rightclickunbound.app).
- Target platform: Chrome only.
- Audience: casual users and power users.

## Messaging drafts
- Popup hint: "Enable for this site" and "Force Mode for stubborn pages."
- Settings note: "Some sites may require a reload after enabling."
- Store short description: "Unlock copy and right-click on sites that block it."

## MVP acceptance criteria
- One-click enable on current site.
- Force Mode toggle available.
- Per-site list shows enable/force entries.
- No telemetry or analytics.
- Clear permissions rationale.

## Phase 0 outputs
- Product brief (this repo: `PRODUCT_BRIEF.md`).
- Roadmap draft (next phase target dates and scope).
- Store copy draft and asset list (screenshots, icons).
