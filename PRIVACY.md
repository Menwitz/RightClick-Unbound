# Privacy Policy

RightClick Unbound does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

## What is stored
The extension stores your enabled site list and last-enabled timestamps locally in Chrome storage so it can remember your preferences.

## What is not collected
- No browsing history is collected or transmitted.
- No analytics or tracking is used.
- No data is shared with third parties.

## Contact
If you have questions, email support@rightclickunbound.app.
